About Me

I am a passionate and hardworking Bachelor of Information Systems student in Telkom University Bandung. I enjoy learning new things and always try my best in carrying out my duties. At that time I was introduced to the basics of HTML and CSS. Understanding the basic structure of a web page and providing a visual layout with CSS provides unexpected satisfaction.
